Perfect for first-timers! Enjoy a gentle 3-hour float through scenic canyons with Class I-II rapids. Our experienced guides ensure a safe, memorable introduction to rafting.

  • Difficulty: Class V
  • Price: $350 per person
  • Min Age: 16 years
